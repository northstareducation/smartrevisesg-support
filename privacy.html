Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 18, 2026

Summary: We collect minimal data necessary to provide our educational service. We never sell your data. Children's privacy is our top priority.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide our educational services:

  • Account Information: Email address and name for account creation
  • Student Profiles: Name, grade level, and selected subjects
  • Learning Data: Answers to practice questions, progress, and performance metrics
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns to improve our service

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide personalized learning experiences
  • Track and display learning progress
  • Power our AI Tutor with context about learning needs
  • Send important account notifications
  • Improve our educational content and features

3. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

StudySmartSG is designed for children under 13 with parental supervision:

  • Parent/guardian email is required for account creation
  • We do not collect more information than necessary
  • Parents can review, modify, or delete their child's data
  • We do not display targeted advertising to children
  • AI interactions are filtered for age-appropriate content

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Data is stored securely on Amazon Web Services (AWS)
  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Passwords are securely hashed and never stored in plain text
  • We implement industry-standard security measures

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • AWS (Amazon Web Services): Cloud infrastructure and data storage
  • AWS Bedrock (Claude AI): Powers our AI Tutor feature
  • Apple Sign In: Optional authentication method

We do not share personal data with advertisers or data brokers.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained while your account is active
  • Learning progress data is retained for educational continuity
  • You can request data deletion at any time
  • Deleted accounts are permanently removed within 30 days

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your learning data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

8. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights:

  • Email: privacy@studysmartsg.com
  • Feedback Form: Submit a request

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification.
